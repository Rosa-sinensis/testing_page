The AHA! Moment

The story starts when I’m at my home country, Malaysia, far away from my study place, taking time off enjoying summer holiday after a very hectic year.

When you have much free time, one thing that I feel I always need to do is a reflection.

I do realize I have a problem in documenting/recording my journey. I have done a lot of projects over my first two year as undergraduates, yet I barely able to remember it. - I really hate that feeling.

Going through my project, most of them has something to do with analytical; instrumentation, electronics, machine learning, mathematics - little has to do with creative site.

And then AHA!!!, It's a great learning opportunity for me to sharpen my creativity, while started compiling my past projects.
